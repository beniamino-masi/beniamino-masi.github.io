About Me

I am a Post-doctoral Researcher at the University of Pisa (since December 2025), currently working on the research project WATERSENS. My work lies at the intersection of comparative politics, party organization, and European policy.

Previously, I served as a Post-doctoral Researcher at the University of Padua (2023–2025), working on the project "Governing the 'Just Transition': Eco-social Politics and Policies in the EU".

I received my PhD in Political Science from the University of Pisa in 2024, with a thesis titled "Patterns of party organizational change. Convergence and disintermediation in Europe (1969-2017)".

My research interests include the evolution of political parties in Western Europe, intra-party democracy, and the political implications of the green transition. I also serve as the Communication Officer for the "Political Parties, Party Systems and Elections" Research Network of the Council for European Studies.
